Focaccia

Total time: 45 mins | Difficulty: Easy | Vegan Friendly

This fluffy and flavorful Italian bread is perfect as a snack or a side.

For the Dough:

4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour

1 ½ cups (360ml) warm water (about 110°F / 43°C)

2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast

2 tsp salt

2 tbsp olive oil (for the dough)

Topping & Baking:

2–3 tbsp olive oil (for drizzling)

sea salt (for sprinkling)

Fresh rosemary (or herbs of choice)

thinly sliced garlic, cherry tomatoes, olives, or caramelized onions

Spicy Pasta

Total time: 35 mins | Difficulty: Easy | Vegan Friendly

A quick and fiery pasta dish to liven up your dinner table.

For the Pasta:

8 oz (225g) pasta (spaghetti, penne, or any preferred pasta)

1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking the pasta)

For the Sauce:

2 tbsp olive oil (for sautéing)

3–4 garlic cloves, minced

1–2 red chili peppers (or 1 tsp red pepper flakes) – adjust to your spice preference

1 can (14 oz / 400g) diced tomatoes (or 2 cups fresh tomatoes, chopped)

1 tbsp tomato paste (optional, for deeper flavor)

1 tsp sugar (to balance acidity)

Salt and pepper to taste

For Garnish:

Fresh basil leaves or parsley

Grated Parmesan cheese

Fattoush Salad

Total time: 35 mins | Difficulty: Easy | Gluten Free

A crunchy and refreshing Lebanese salad, packed with herbs and vegetables.

4 cups mixed greens (such as Romaine lettuce, arugula, or spinach)

1 cucumber

2 medium tomatoes

1 small red onion, thinly sliced

1 bell pepper

1 cup radishes, thinly sliced

2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped

For the Dressing:

3 tbsp olive oil

2 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp pomegranate molasses

t tsp ground sumac

salt and pepper for taste

For a Crunchy Topping:

2-3 pieces of pita bread
